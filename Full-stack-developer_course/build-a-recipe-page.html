Spicy Baked Omelet

This bold and savory baked omelet is a great choice for a hearty breakfast, a weekend brunch, or a quick evening meal.

What You'll Need

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 cup diced cooked ham
  • 1 (10 oz) can of Rotel (drained)
  • 8 oz shredded Colby and Monterey Jack cheese blend (or your favorite cheese)

How to Make It

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Lightly coat an 8.5 x 8.5 inch baking dish with nonstick spray.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper until slightly frothy.
  4. Add in the chopped ham, drained Rotel, and shredded cheese. Stir to combine.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and bake for 25–30 minutes, or until fully set.
  6. Once baked, turn off the oven and let the dish sit inside for an additional 5–10 minutes to finish cooking gently.
